How it works

We do this one step at a time.

You stay in control the whole way. Here's how it usually goes.

1

Free call. Free

We hop on a call. You tell me what's eating up your time and what your team already uses. We figure out together whether there's something here worth fixing.

2

A written fit and price.

If it looks like a fit, I write down what I'd fix first, what's included, what's not, and what it costs. You look it over and decide before we do anything.

3

You approve the information.

Once you're good with the plan, you only share the information you're comfortable with me using. We write down exactly what that includes.

4

A test or shadow run.

I set up the first task and run it quietly next to how you do things now. That lets us compare the results before it touches a real customer.

5

You accept it.

You review the results and give the okay. If something's off, we fix it. Nothing goes into regular use until you're happy with it.

6

Launch.

The approved task goes into regular use. Your team gets a short, plain-English guide so they can run it without me.

7

Monthly review. Monthly

After that, we check in once a month. I keep things running, check the output, update your guide when something changes, and bring you the next improvement to approve. Bigger projects get quoted separately before we start.

Common questions

Straight answers before you book.

Is this just another subscription?

No. You are paying for finished business work and for keeping it running, not for a login you have to manage. What the monthly covers is written in your scope.

Will my staff have to learn a complicated new system?

The goal is less for your team to do, not more. The AI runs behind the scenes, work reaches your team as drafts to check, and anything they do touch comes with a short plain-English guide.

What does the setup fee pay for?

The one-time setup pays for mapping the task, putting your approved information in place, writing the approval rules, building the first working setup, testing it, and giving you a handoff guide. You keep those results.

Why is there a monthly fee at all?

Because the work keeps coming. New requests arrive, tools change, and output still needs checking. The monthly is finished work plus upkeep. It is not unlimited AI work; what is included is in your scope.

What will you have access to?

Only what the agreed job needs, and only what you approve. We write down what you share before work starts, and you can change it. I work inside the tools you already own.

Do I own the work, and what happens if we stop?

The setup is built inside your own accounts and tools, so the working task, your information, and the written guide stay with you. Exactly how pausing or ending works is confirmed in your written scope before you start, so there are no surprises.

Are third-party software costs included?

Those are separate. If a task needs paid third-party software, the cost is yours and is listed and approved before anything starts. I will not sign you up for anything without your okay.

AI makes mistakes. How do I know I can trust the output?

You do not have to trust it blindly. Important output is prepared as a draft for a person to check, and the approval rules decide what may go out on its own. When something is uncertain, it waits for you.

How much do I actually have to approve?

You approve the information used, the rules, the first results, and any change to what goes out. The routine drafting comes off your plate. The judgment calls stay yours.

Can you handle sensitive or private work?

Anything sensitive is handled only under rules we agree on in writing, and it waits for a person before it goes anywhere. If you would rather keep certain work fully manual, we leave it out of scope.

When do I need a custom quote?

Custom apps, integrations, websites, customer portals, and anything unusually large or sensitive are quoted separately. You see the scope and price in writing before that work starts.
