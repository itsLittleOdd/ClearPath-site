About Justin and ClearPath

I'm Justin. I've spent years in hospitality, events, and Holiday Valley, so I've been on the receiving end of bad workflows: missed calls during a rush, BEOs that don't match the floor, schedules built in three different apps. ClearPath is what I do now: find the one workflow costing an owner the most time, then fix it in plain English.
